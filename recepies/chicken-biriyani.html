Chicken Biriyani Recipe


Home

Description

Chicken Biriyani is a classic Indian rice dish made with layers of fragrant basmati rice, marinated chicken, and a blend of aromatic spices. This flavorful and hearty meal is often served at celebrations and gatherings. The chicken is cooked with spices and yogurt, then layered with rice and cooked to perfection. Serve with raita or salad.


Ingredients

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 500g chicken, cut into pieces
  • 1 cup plain yogurt
  • 2 onions, thinly sliced
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 teaspoons biriyani masala
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • Whole spices (4 cloves, 4 cardamoms, 1 bay leaf, 1-inch cinnamon stick)
  • 1/4 cup chopped cilantro
  • 1/4 cup chopped mint leaves
  • 3 tablespoons ghee or oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Water as needed

Steps

  1. Wash and soak the basmati rice for 30 minutes. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a bowl, marinate the chicken with yogurt, turmeric, red chili powder, and salt. Set aside for 30 minutes.
  3. Heat ghee or oil in a large pot. Add whole spices and sauté until fragrant. Add sliced onions and cook until golden brown.
  4. Add ginger-garlic paste and sauté for 1-2 minutes. Add chopped tomatoes and cook until soft.
  5. Add marinated chicken, biriyani masala, and garam masala. Cook until the chicken is done and the oil separates.
  6. In a separate pot, boil water with salt. Add soaked rice and cook until 70% done. Drain the rice.
  7. Layer the cooked chicken with rice, sprinkling cilantro and mint between layers. Cover and cook on low heat for 20 minutes.
  8. Let it rest for 10 minutes before serving. Fluff the rice gently and serve hot.
