Indian Style Crab Roast Recipe


Home

Description

Indian Style Crab Roast is a spicy and flavorful seafood dish popular in coastal regions of India. Fresh crab is cooked with a blend of aromatic spices, onions, tomatoes, and coconut, resulting in a rich and delicious roast. It is best enjoyed with steamed rice or traditional Indian breads.


Ingredients

  • 4 medium-sized crabs, cleaned and cut
  • 2 tablespoons coconut oil
  • 2 large onions, thinly sliced
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 green chilies, slit
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 2 teaspoons red chili powder
  • 1 tablespoon coriander powder
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1/2 cup grated coconut
  • 10-12 curry leaves
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves, for garnish

Steps

  1. Heat coconut oil in a large pan over medium heat. Add sliced onions and sauté until golden brown.
  2. Add ginger-garlic paste, green chilies, and curry leaves. Sauté for 2 minutes until fragrant.
  3. Add chopped tomatoes and cook until they turn soft and oil separates.
  4. Add turmeric powder, red chili powder, coriander powder, and black pepper powder. Mix well and cook for 2-3 minutes.
  5. Add the cleaned crab pieces and salt. Mix to coat the crab with the masala.
  6. Cover and cook for 10-12 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the crab is cooked through.
  7. Add grated coconut and garam masala. Roast on high heat for 3-4 minutes until the masala thickens and coats the crab.
  8. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot.
